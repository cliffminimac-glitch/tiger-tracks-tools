About the Role

This is a rare seat at the table. As Executive Assistant to our CEO, you'll operate at the center of a high-growth agency — not just managing logistics, but actively shaping how the business runs. You'll have visibility into strategy, clients, finance, and operations that most people take years to access.

We're looking for someone who thinks like a Chief of Staff and executes like an operator. If you're energized by ownership, thrive when no two days are the same, and take pride in making a fast-moving leader more effective — this role was built for you.

What You'll Own

Executive Presence & Communications

  • Serve as the CEO's first point of contact — managing calendar, inbox, and communications with discretion and speed
  • Anticipate needs before they're voiced: prep materials, briefings, and talking points ahead of every meeting
  • Keep the CEO focused on what matters by filtering, prioritizing, and resolving before things escalate

Business Development & Client Operations

  • Partner with the BD team to keep the pipeline moving — tracking deals, coordinating follow-ups, and preparing client-facing materials
  • Draft communications, prepare decks, and coordinate cross-functional agendas that reflect the company's standards

Finance & Business Operations

  • Own the invoicing and accounting workflow end-to-end — from monthly billing through reconciliation and collections
  • Maintain financial accuracy and documentation that the business can rely on at audit time or at any board meeting

Team & Company Coordination

  • Manage internal operations: onboarding, Asana, team milestones, and the connective tissue that keeps the org running smoothly
  • Plan and execute company events, executive dinners, and team retreats that reflect who Tiger Tracks is

Who You Are

  • A proven operator: 3+ years supporting a C-level executive or founder in a fast-moving environment
  • Ownership-oriented: You don't wait to be asked — you identify what needs to happen and make it happen
  • A skilled communicator: You write clearly, represent an executive's voice with confidence, and know when to speak and when to listen
  • Financially fluent: Comfortable with invoicing, reconciliation, QuickBooks, and the basics of contract and vendor management
  • Discreet and trustworthy: You understand that access to sensitive information is a privilege, not a perk
  • Energized by pace: You do your best work when the stakes are high and the environment is moving fast
