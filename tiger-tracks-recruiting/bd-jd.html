The Role

You will own the full revenue cycle from first conversation to signed contract. This is a pipeline-building role — you are expected to proactively source opportunities through outbound, referrals, and partnerships, not rely on inbound leads being handed to you. You will build and present paid media audits to diagnose performance gaps and identify growth opportunities. You are not responsible for ongoing campaign execution.

The best fit for this role has usually followed a path like: Paid Media Manager → Senior Strategist → Growth Director → Director of Partnerships. You can run a real audit, talk strategy at the executive level, and close deals. You are equal parts technical expert, hunter, and trusted advisor.
