The Role

As a Senior Account Manager, you are the client steward and people leader for your pod. You own day-to-day relationships for Tier 2-3 clients, support leadership on Tier 1 accounts, and ensure consistency, quality, and a strong client experience across your book of business. You are the first escalation point and a critical driver of retention, growth, and team development.
