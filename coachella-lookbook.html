Palm Springs Heat Guide — April

Best FabricsLinen, chiffon, rayon, viscose, silk, poplin. Avoid velvet, cashmere, mohair, heavy wool.
Color LogicLight colors reflect heat. Dark absorbs 10–15°F extra. Save dark looks for night.
Silhouette = AirflowWide-leg trousers create a chimney effect. Fringe creates its own breeze. Mini = minimum heat trap.
Open-Toe Only (Day)Closed shoes in 100°F = misery. Boots are fine after 8pm when temps drop.
Hat is Non-NegotiableWide-brim hat drops personal temperature by 10°F. UV400 sunglasses always.
Evening RuleCards with amber border = best after 6pm at 70–75°F. Feathers, velvet, denim = evening only.

SS26 Trend Glossary

Key trends driving the 2026 Coachella lookbook. Sources: Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, PopSugar.

Fringe Everything
Loulou de Saison · Isabel Marant · Dries Van Noten
The #1 SS26 festival trend. Fringe creates its own breeze in Palm Springs heat. Skirts, bags, scarves, boots.
Scarf Tops & Headwraps
Vince · Tory Burch · Gap
Silk bandanas worn as tops, hair wraps, or neck scarves. Lightweight and sun-protective.
Statement Skirts
Toteme · Khaite · 16Arlington
Bold, sculptural skirts with volume. Pairs with simple tanks in the heat.
Sequins & Feathers
Area · Retrofete · Calvin Klein
Nighttime power moves. At 70°F desert nights, feathers and sequins feel exactly right.
Decorated Waists
Ashlynn · Diotima · Brandon Maxwell
Embellished belts, corsets, and waist details. Harper's Bazaar #1 trend.
Bold Red Outerwear
Ralph Lauren · WARDROBE.NYC
After midnight layers when desert temps finally drop. Evening/late night only in April.
Animal Print
Sergio Hudson · Dehanche · Zara
Leopard print at full volume. Wear open as a layer for maximum breathability in the heat.
Lapis Blue + Jewel Tones
Cotton Citizen · Bottega Veneta · Dior
The SS26 color story. Lapis, sapphire, amethyst, emerald. Light jewel tones are cooler than darks.
Hot Pants & Micro Denim
Lioness · LaQuan Smith · Area
Minimum fabric = maximum cool. Literally the most heat-logical festival look ever.
Pom-Poms
Luar · Calvin Klein · Area
PopSugar #1 accessory trend. Lightweight on bags and shoes. Adds movement without warmth.
Linen & Chiffon
Every brand worth mentioning
The Palm Springs fabrics. Everything else in daytime is secondary. Linen absorbs 20% moisture without feeling wet.
Balloon Silhouettes
Michael Kors · Ashlyn · Campillo
Volume dressing at night. Works when the temperature drops and you can afford extra fabric.
