Ongoing: Annual Comp Review Framework

  • Q1 each year: Market data refresh (PayScale, BuiltIn, agency salary surveys). Update bands if market has shifted.
  • Q1 each year: Performance reviews drive band positioning. High performers move up within band; low performers stay or are managed out.
  • Budget 3-5% of payroll annually for merit increases (~$90K-$150K at current headcount). This keeps pace with market inflation.
  • Promotion budget separate from merit: Reserve 2-3% of payroll (~$60K-$90K) specifically for promotions.
  • Band compression checks: Every 6 months, audit for managers earning less than their reports or peers with >15% gaps at the same level.
  • New hire calibration: Never hire below the 25th percentile of a band. Anchor new hires at 30-50% of band to leave growth room.
